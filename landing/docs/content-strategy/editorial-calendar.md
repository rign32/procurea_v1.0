## 1. Ordering rationale

We sequence the 20 posts in three waves, not by pillar rotation or by Notion publish date order. The logic:

1. **Wave 1 (Weeks 1–4)** — launch with the 5 highest-leverage TOFU posts that drive organic traffic and feed the MQL/Welcome nurtures on day one. These are the posts that are already linked by N1/N2/W1/W2, so without them the email sequences have broken links.
2. **Wave 2 (Weeks 5–12)** — layer in MOFU comparison content (vendor scoring, AI features buyer's guide, TCO, China+1 playbook) plus the second-wave case study amplifiers. This is where we start converting newsletter subscribers to demo bookings.
3. **Wave 3 (Weeks 13–24)** — BOFU / integration / vertical depth (SAP S/4HANA, NetSuite, Salesforce, textile vertical, German manufacturer deep dive). These posts serve the P1 pipeline and defend our commercial-intent keywords.

**Key constraint**: every post in Wave 1 is referenced by either MQL Nurture N1–N6 or Welcome W1–W6. If Wave 1 slips, the email sequences cannot ship. That is why Wave 1 is 2-week launch urgency, not nice-to-have.

## 9. Publishing checklist per post (repeated from strategy.md Definition of Done)

Every post before going live on procurea.io:

- [ ] Primary keyword + 2–5 secondary keywords from `keyword-map.md`
- [ ] H1 = primary KW (exact or natural variant)
- [ ] 3 H2 sections minimum, each containing at least one secondary KW
- [ ] Primary CTA matches Notion `Primary CTA` field (e.g., "Start free with 10 credits")
- [ ] Hero image (PNG, ≥1600×900) — credits: UX Designer → Graphic Designer
- [ ] OG image (1200×630, distinct from hero, includes post title + Procurea logo)
- [ ] Inline CTA block (middle of post) linking to lead magnet OR signup
- [ ] Related Posts footer (3 related by pillar or persona)
- [ ] Linked to ≥3 emails in Notion (editable by Content Marketing Manager)
- [ ] Linked to ≥2 LinkedIn posts in Notion `🎤 LinkedIn Pipeline`
- [ ] Preview on procurea-app-staging review URL
- [ ] PL translation drafted + reviewed against PL persona voice notes
- [ ] SEO Specialist sign-off (keyword density, internal links)
- [ ] Senior Marketing Manager sign-off (anti-patterns check, voice consistency)

See `feedback-loop-protocol.md` for the agent responsibilities behind each checkbox.
