# Procurea Content Hub — Editorial Roadmap

> Owner: Product Owner (content)
> Last updated: 2026-04-18
> Scope: 20 blog posts, 5 lead magnets, 5 case study skeletons across `procurea.io/resources` (EN) and `procurea.pl/materialy` (PL). EN-first.

---

## Vision (1 paragraph)

Procurea's content hub is the operator's reference library for modern B2B procurement teams navigating a post-globalization supply chain. We do not publish listicles or vague "AI will change sourcing" essays. Every asset teaches a repeatable workflow, ships a tool (template, calculator, checklist), or proves a specific outcome with numbers — grounded in the reality of a Purchasing Manager losing 30 hours per campaign to Excel, a Head of Procurement defending vendor choices to the board, or a founder needing 20 qualified suppliers before Friday. The hub converts readers into 10-credit trials (P2/P3) and integration conversations (P1) by being the single most useful place on the internet to learn procurement from practitioners, not consultants.

---

## Prioritization rationale

Three filters, applied in order:

1. **Search demand × conversion proximity** — we rank pieces with ≥500/mo search volume AND a clear product tie-in above thin MOFU/BOFU posts that depend on already-aware readers.
2. **Flagship durability** — Wave 1 must be evergreen, re-shareable, and defensible if a competitor copies the angle within 60 days. We avoid posts where our only angle is "Procurea does this."
3. **Lead magnet pairing** — 4 of 5 Wave 1 posts ship alongside or point to a paired lead magnet. Content + downloadable asset = the reliable newsletter opt-in flywheel.

### Seasonal notes
- **Q2 2026 (current)**: Nearshoring, EU sourcing, China+1 all peak now — procurement budgets reset, Q2 RFQ cycles spike. Front-load Waves 1-2 with nearshoring/multilingual content.
- **Q3 2026**: Budget planning begins August-September — TCO, vendor scoring, supplier master data cleanup all spike. Wave 3 carries these.
- **Q4 2026**: ERP comparison content (SAP, NetSuite, Salesforce) ranks better as buyers finalize 2027 stacks. Integration BOFU posts land late.

## Workflow handoff

1. Product Owner (this doc) produces briefs — this folder.
2. UX Copywriter writes draft per brief, EN-first. Owns tone + structure.
3. SEO Specialist reviews: KW density, meta title/description, FAQ schema, internal links.
4. UX Designer: hero layout, inline diagrams, CTA placement, mobile check.
5. Graphic Designer: feature image, social cards (1200x630), any infographics flagged in brief.
6. PO final review → ship to staging → EN live → PL translation (Gemini + human review).
