## Copy angle (1 sentence)
Every sourcing tool demo looks the same — this post ships the 12 questions that separate real from cosmetic capability (data sources, LLM transparency, integration depth, pricing honesty, customer proof) and tells you the disqualifying answers to listen for.

## Must-include elements
- The 12 questions organized in 4 themes:
  - **Data sources (3)**: Which web sources do you actually crawl? Do you scrape directories or license data? How do you handle non-English sources?
  - **AI + transparency (3)**: Which LLM do you use and is it swappable? Can I see the reasoning for each supplier's relevance score? What's your precision/recall on a category like mine?
  - **Integration + workflow (3)**: Which ERPs have productized (not "we can build it") connectors? CSV export schema? API access on which plan?
  - **Commercials + customer proof (3)**: Pricing transparent on site? Credit-based or seat-based? Show me 3 live customers in my size range.
- For each question: the "good answer," the "yellow flag," the "walk away" answer. Be specific.
- Scorecard template: 12 questions × 3-point scale = 36 max. Minimum viable score: 24. Explain why < 24 is a no, and why a perfect 36 is suspicious (vendor said yes to everything).
- The honesty test: ask every vendor "what's the #1 thing your product is NOT good at?" If they can't answer, walk away.
- The 3-customer reference check: not a logo slide, actual customer calls. 30 min each. Ask about the failure modes.
- Procurea's self-answers: we can and do answer all 12. Readers get the Procurea scorecard inline.
