## Must-include elements
- Honest positioning: Procurea complements NetSuite for pre-PO sourcing and qualification. NetSuite remains your Vendor master, PO authority, and payment system. Link to `/integrations`.
- What NetSuite's Vendor record does well: 1099 tracking, multi-currency, tax compliance, payment workflow, Vendor Bills.
- What it doesn't do: supplier discovery, RFQ orchestration, multilingual outreach, certification expiry tracking, supplier risk scoring, structured quote comparison.
- The 3 integration approaches: (1) SuiteCloud CSV import of new vendors (day-one, manual cadence), (2) SuiteTalk REST + Merge.dev managed sync (pilot), (3) custom SuiteScript for real-time sync (Enterprise Custom).
- A realistic field-mapping table: Procurea supplier card → NetSuite Vendor fields (Company Name → companyname, Tax ID → taxidnum, Primary Contact → contactlist, Category → category).
- Decision tree: when to buy NetSuite Procurement vs. SuiteApp from Boomi/Celigo vs. best-of-breed + integration.
