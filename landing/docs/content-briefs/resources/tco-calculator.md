## Copy angle (1 sentence)
The TCO model that turns a "3% unit-price savings" into a "12% TCO loss" — with 7 hidden cost categories pre-calculated so Finance can't dismiss it as soft numbers.

## Must-include elements (in the asset itself)
- **Tab 1 — Inputs**: per-supplier unit price, MOQ, lead time (days), payment terms (Net X), origin country, known defect rate %, freight cost per unit, required tooling cost, certifications status.
- **Tab 2 — Calculation**: formulas that compute landed cost, inventory carrying cost (extended lead time × annual demand × carrying rate), defect cost (defect rate × rework cost), payment-terms cost (DSO impact × cost of capital), switching cost (tooling amortization), risk premium (single-source penalty).
- **Tab 3 — Summary + Chart**: side-by-side TCO per supplier with a bar chart showing nominal unit price vs. fully-loaded TCO. Winner highlighted.
- **README tab**: how to use, default assumptions (cost of capital 8%, carrying rate 20%, defect rework cost 3× unit), where to adjust.
- Professional formatting: no pastel colors, CFO-grade, printable A4 landscape.

## Format
XLSX, Microsoft Excel 2016+ compatible, Google Sheets compatible. File size < 300 KB. No macros (enterprise IT blocks macro-enabled files).

## Value props (5 bullets for landing page)

- Works for 2-5 suppliers, product or service categories.
