# Keyword Database — 20 Blog Posts

> **Purpose**: per-post SEO intake sheet. Primary KW, secondaries, SERP intent check, PAA capture, competing domains, angle recommendation. Feed this into H-structure + meta templates.
>
> **Conventions**: Intent = I (informational), C (commercial), T (transactional). Volumes are **rough proxies** (no paid KW tool; GSC baseline after 30-60 days). All KW in US English (procurea.io flagship).
>
> **Opinionated rule**: when SERP is dominated by templates-sites (rfqtemplate.com, smartsheet.com) or enterprise vendor pages (coupa.com, sap.com), we **don't fight them head-on** — we take an adjacent angle (comparison, practitioner POV, specific geography) and funnel in via long-tails.
