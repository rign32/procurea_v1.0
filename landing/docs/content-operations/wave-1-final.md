## Posts I am rejecting from Wave 1 (and why)

### `supplier-risk-management-checklist-2026` (PO Wave 1 → moved to Wave 3 #16)

- **Volume is real** (1,500, P1, CSDDD 2026 angle).
- **Problem 1**: **High KD**. SERP is owned by Prewave, Riskmethods, Dun & Bradstreet, Deloitte, Gartner (keyword-database.md line 298). Wave 1 should front-load quick wins that establish topical authority, not initiate a 90-day uphill battle against enterprise incumbents on day one. With DR 0 on launch, we will not rank top-20 for this term in Q2.
- **Problem 2**: **Cross-channel payoff is thin in Wave 1**. Only 1 email references it in the first 30 days: P1-04 at Day 14. The P1 Nurture sequence runs for users who have already converted — meaning this post serves retention/nurture, not acquisition. Wave 1 needs acquisition posts.
- **Problem 3**: **Depends on CSDDD content being fresh**. If we ship in April, we're either (a) writing before Q2 regulatory news cycles spike or (b) competing with EU Commission + Deloitte CSDDD think-pieces that will ship in Q2. Better to land this in Wave 3 (August 2026) when regulatory intent peaks and we have topical authority.
- **Reassignment**: **Wave 3, publish Thu 2026-08-20** (as per SMM editorial calendar #16). No change — SMM already had this correct.

### `ai-procurement-software-7-features-2026` (PO Wave 1 → moved to Wave 2 #10)

- **Volume is real** (900, Mixed persona, commercial intent).
- **Problem 1**: **High KD, enterprise SERP**. Competing against Coupa, GEP, SAP Ariba, Gartner Magic Quadrant. Per keyword-database.md line 219: "We will not rank #1 against Coupa. We can rank #4-8 with honest teardowns." A rank #4-8 is worth pursuing, but it's a 6-month build, not a Wave 1 flagship.
- **Problem 2**: **Content-first dependency**. The "7 features worth paying for" framework presupposes reader context — who is the reader? The post *assumes* the reader has already seen our 30-hour problem frame and the supplier discovery funnel explainer. Shipping this as post 5 in Wave 1 (before Post 2 the 30-hour problem anchor and Post 18 the funnel explainer) means readers land without the scaffolding.
- **Problem 3**: **N3 fires Day 7** — and N3 is the only email this post unblocks in Wave 1. But the MQL Nurture sequence does not start firing until users convert from Wave 1 posts. So we have a circular dependency PO's plan doesn't resolve: MQL signups come FROM Wave 1 posts, so Post 10 (which serves MQL users) doesn't need to ship BEFORE the signups happen. Ship it Wave 2 Jun 15, and N3 still fires in time for the first cohort's Day 7 (average cohort Day 7 = late May / early June).
- **Reassignment**: **Wave 2, publish Mon 2026-06-15** (SMM editorial calendar #10). Confirmed.
