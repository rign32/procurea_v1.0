FAQ

Frequent questions

Where does supplier data come from?

A mix of public registries, indexed trade directories, scraped OEM/distributor catalogs, partner verification firms, and our own re-verification loop. Every record shown to you is less than 90 days old.

Can I bring my own supplier list?

Yes. Upload a CSV of your incumbents and we'll merge them with our index so you can compare like-for-like.

Is my RFQ data shared with suppliers?

Only the fields you mark as shareable. Internal notes, budget ceilings and comparison scores stay with you.

Which regions & categories are covered?

United States, Canada, United Kingdom and Australia today across 12 categories (manufacturing, construction, packaging, MRO, logistics, electronics, plastics, metals, chemicals, textiles, food-grade, facilities).

Does it work with my ERP?

CSV export works with any system. Native connectors for NetSuite, QuickBooks Enterprise, Microsoft Dynamics 365 BC, Sage Intacct, Acumatica, Xero.
