# Cover Design Brief — Supplier Risk Checklist 2026

For the graphic designer converting the Markdown manuscript into a print-ready PDF.

## Format

- A4, portrait, 6-8 physical pages (after layout — the Markdown yields ~12 pages of content; target 6 pages by using 2-column layout on the 20 checks and a smaller font on the reference tables)
- PDF/X-1a for print compatibility
- File size target: under 500 KB
- Print-friendly: avoid full-bleed backgrounds, use 15mm margins
- Accessibility: real text (not rasterised), selectable, searchable

## Cover page

**Hero visual concept:** A stylised grid of 20 squares in a 4×5 matrix. Each square represents one verification check. Eight squares are filled in the Procurea blue, twelve are outlined. The partially-filled state communicates "checklist in progress" — operator, not theoretical.

**Palette:**
- Primary: Procurea blue (use existing brand hex from the landing site — probably #2563EB or similar)
- Secondary: charcoal (#1F2937) for body text
- Accent: amber (#F59E0B) for red-flag callouts
- Background: off-white (#FAFAFA), not pure white — easier on print

**Typography:**
- Cover title: Geist Bold 48pt or Inter ExtraBold 44pt
- Subtitle: Geist Regular 18pt
- Body: Inter Regular 10pt with 14pt line-height
- Table headers: Inter Semibold 10pt

**Cover copy (keep these exact lines):**

> **Supplier Risk Checklist 2026**
>
> The 20-Point Verification Guide for Procurement Teams
>
> 5 risk dimensions · 20 checks · auto-reject thresholds · re-check cadences
>
> Published by Procurea · April 2026

## Page templates

- Section opener pages (Financial, Operational, etc.): full-width banner in that section's accent colour with the dimension name, a one-line description, and the check numbers it contains.
- Check pages: two-column layout where the left column holds "What to check" + "Source of truth" and the right holds "Red flag" + "Re-check cadence." Each check gets a numbered badge in the outer margin.
- The red-flag summary matrix (page 10) should be a full-width table with alternating row shading.
- The printable one-page version (final page): absolutely clean, high-contrast, black and white, no gradients — it must photocopy cleanly.

## Back cover

- Procurea logo bottom-right
- Tagline: "From 30 hours to 20 minutes. 100-250 verified suppliers across 26 languages."
- URL: procurea.io/resources/supplier-risk-checklist
- Small print: "Free to copy and adapt. No attribution required. Revised semiannually."

## Icon system (optional but recommended)

Commission or license a 20-icon set, one per check. Line-art, two-weight, consistent grid. Use:
- Scales icon for legal entity
- Coins / chart for financial
- Factory / gauge for operational
- Shield for sanctions
- Leaf for ESG
- Padlock for cyber

If budget constrained, use Tabler Icons (MIT licensed) and skin them in brand blue.

## Don'ts

- No stock photos of handshakes, warehouses, or "diverse business team" imagery. Operators find these insulting.
- No gradient meshes or glassmorphism effects. This is a reference document, not a landing page.
- No em-dashes on the cover. One em-dash per page max inside.
- Do not add "A Procurea whitepaper" framing — the document is self-contained and positions Procurea in the footer only.
